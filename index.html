Iperin Annual Race

Iperin Annual Race is just around the corner! This year we have a lot of participants
So here's how our registration will work. There are adult runners (over 18 years of age) and youth runners (under 18 years of age)

Race number:

  • Male adults receive a race number at or above 1000.
  • Female adults and youths receive a race number below 1000.

Start time:

  • Male Adults registrants run at 9:30am
  • Female Adults registrants run at 11:00am
  • Youth registrants run at 12:30am(regardless of gender).
